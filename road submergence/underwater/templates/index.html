Welcome to Road-Submergence site!

One of the primary task of Road & Building Department of Government of Gujarat department is to provide village connectivity to all revenue villages and their habitations with all road network to connect them with Taluka/District Head Quarters. This road connectivity ensures the services like Health, Education, Social Services, Agricultural product marketing etc. to all the citizens residing in rural areas.

What are the Problems Faced?

1

The roads get submerged and the villages/ habitations are inaccessible for many days resulting into untold hardship to villages and they cut off with all basic services.

2

Submerged roads cause deterioration of roads by the rains. Several heavy vehicles got stuck on the waterlogged roads, and several two-wheeler riders fell while traversing the damaged roads.

3

Due to rainfall and heavy water discharge from the dams often problem of water logging occurs ,which ultimately causes many epidemic diseases.

4

Water supply and electricity are disrupted and people struggle and suffer as a result.

5

Animals die in flash floods, sometime insects and snakes make their ways to the area and cause a lot of havoc.

Solution?

A composite Machine Learning Model is implemented for forecasting the Rainfall and Dam Water Discharge. The Machine Learning model used is ARIMA which is highly accurate and best reccomended for time series data i.e. data collected over regular time intervals.We are using approx past 65 years data currently and it increases year by year as new data is collected in the same fashion. The combined predicted effect of Rainfall and Dam Water Discharge is accounted for predicting the Road Submergence . The Road submergence is predicted area-wise(Tahseels/Provinces/District) taking in account the local conditions i.e Rainfall,Dam Water Discharge and Area along with locally manuvering the right Level for Submergence.
